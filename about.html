What is the Open Exchange for Social Change?

The Open Exchange for Social Change is the official IODC unconference. The unconference will be an open and safe space for you to exchange thoughts,ideas and knowledge with your peers . Our primary focus of the event will be on how open data creates, contributes and supports social change. We want to better understand the actions we need to take to increase positive impact in our communities. Your ideas and actions will be feed into the main conference, and help us to set the goals for the IODC roadmap.

What is an Unconference?

An Unconference is a method where the agenda for the event is set up by the attendees at the beginning of the day. This event design philosophy and facilitation focuses on maximizing collaboration and peer sharing. We invite any participant to propose and facilitate a session. Participants can attend a session, or move to another sessions as they see fit. We hope to maximise the collective untapped knowledge and experience of the participants, and we strive to tap that vast store by maximizing dialog, creativity, idea exchange, and the ability to build solidarity.

See Become a Buddy below for more information on proposing a session and also the other ways that you can contribute to The Open Exchange.

Who can participate?

Anyone who is attending the IODC conference is welcome to join The Open Exchange- Civil Society, Journalists, Business and Governments. You can be a newbie for Open Data or and old veteran, all are welcome to contribute to the discussion! In order to participate you must make sure that you will be in Madrid during October 4. Sadly we can't provide travel support.

Who is behind the Open Exchange for Social Change?

The Open Exchange for Social Change unconference is an official pre-event of the International Open Data Conference. It is led by Open Knowledge International, with the assistant of IODC steering committee members from IDRC, The Web Foundation, ILDA and Civica Digital. Dirk Slater from FabRiders will be the Lead Facilitator for the event

Learn facilitation skills!

Creating an open exchange is a challenging task! We, therefore, decided to share our knowledge about unconference facilitation by creating skillshare sessions before the event. In these online sessions , you will learn facilitation skills that you can use not only in the Open Exchange for Social Change but also in your own work.

The buddy system

Can you commit to a couple of hours to help contribute to the success of The Open Exchange? We invite you to help us run this event An unconference is a group effort, and we need your skills and input to make it a success! You can us help by:

  • Leading/Facilitating a session
  • Supporting new facilitators to run sessions
  • Help to document the event and session outcomes
  • Making sure that the unconference will feed the main conference by using your social media skills

To support your contribution as a Buddy, we will be providing a two hour online session where we will provide an overview of The Open Exchange, and tips on Facilitation.
